Groups

  • A maximum number of 5 people per group is allowed and a minimum of 2
  • A group has to have at any point in time a minimum of 2 to be active
  • An individual can be part of one group only at any point of time
  • The points of the group are summation of the individual points
  • If a user decides to leave a group, he is offered the choice whether the future points he earns for the current calendar month can be added to the current group or not
  • The user leaving the group, his existing points will be part of the group for the month and if he decides "YES" as per the point above, then the points for the month get added
  • Once the user leaves the group, his individual points are not taken into consideration for the next month for this group. He can choose to join another group though, and then his points are added to the new group only from the first date of the next calendar month
  • All this while, the individuals points are still tracked for the individual leader board
  • The creator of the group has only privileges to create a group and people. The other members in the group also can add people but the group size is restricted to a maximum of 5
  • The group creator does not have the permission to eject anyone out
